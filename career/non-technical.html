Admission Counselor

Role and Responsibilities:
  • Lead Conversion: Effectively engage with prospective students via calls, emails, and in-person interactions to convert leads into admissions.
  • Provide detailed information about the institute's courses, programs, and admission processes to prospective students.
  • Counseling & Walk-ins: Welcome and counsel walk-in students, understanding their needs and guiding them toward suitable programs.
  • Offer career guidance and assist students in choosing the right courses based on their interests and career goals.
  • Record Management: Maintain and update accurate student records, including personal details, inquiries, and admission status.
  • Ensure proper documentation and adherence to institute policies during the admission process.
  • Follow-ups & Reporting: Conduct regular follow-ups with potential students to ensure timely admission closures.
  • Prepare and share daily/weekly reports on leads, admissions, and related metrics with the reporting manager.
  • Collaborative Tasks: Assist in organizing and participating in promotional activities like webinars, seminars, and open houses.
  • Handle additional responsibilities and tasks assigned by the reporting manager as needed.
  • Education: Bachelor’s degree
  • Job Type: Part/Full time
  • Experience: Excellent communication and interpersonal skills. Proficiency in MS Office and CRM tools. Prior experience in counseling, sales, or customer service will be an advantage.

  • We seek a proactive and organized Admin Executive to manage our administrative operations and ensure a seamless experience for our trainers and students.

  • Ensure a well-maintained and professional work environment.
  • Fee Follow-ups and Recovery: Track and follow up on pending fees with students.
  • Manage recovery of overdue payments and maintain accurate financial records.
  • Trainer Coordination: Update trainers regarding their assigned batches and schedules.
  • Act as the primary point of contact for trainers, addressing their administrative queries.
  • Batch Management: Maintain and update records of ongoing batches, including schedules, trainer assignments, and student attendance.
  • Ensure all stakeholders are informed of any changes to batch schedules.
  • Student Feedback and Updates: Regularly collect and document feedback from students regarding their courses.
  • Communicate feedback to the relevant teams and take necessary action to improve the student experience.
  • Communication and Reporting: Prepare weekly and monthly reports on administrative activities, batch status, and fee collections.
  • Ensure timely communication of updates to trainers, students, and management.
  • Education: Any Graduate
  • Job Type: Full Time
  • Experience: Min 2 years of experience, Excellent communication skills and interpersonal skills.
